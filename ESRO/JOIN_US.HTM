eIEN Kashmir provides a platform for Environment Professionals and environment organizations to interact with each other, exchange ideas, share all their expertise and innovations to the International Environment Community and empower the confidence that they are not alone... Environment Professionals, students, teachers, Entrepreneurs, and Nature enthusiasts living and working all over the J&K are eligible to become a Primary member of this International Network.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Who can be the member of eIEN Network?

eIEN Kashmir Network is open to people belonging to all the walks of life. Our network consists of people ranging from academicians to field practitioners, from research scholars to grassroots innovators, from technical professionals to green agriculturalists. Any body with a strong commitment for the improvement of local knowledge network, innovations, grassroots creativity, conservation of bio-diversity, natural resource management, augmentation of innovation, intellectual property rights etc. can be the members of eIEN Kashmir Network.

Why should you become the member of the Network?

Unearthing the huge reservoir of local knowledge that lies with the traditional and grassroots knowledge holders can help India fasten the pace of achieving the status of knowledge leader and a creative society. You can contribute in your own way.

 

How much time, can I give for the Network?

Time is certainly not the criteria for joining hands with the Network. What matters is the burning desire on your part to take Kashmir step forward by putting the whole range of issues pertaining to traditional knowledge, grassroots innovations, bio- diversity, entrepreneurship etc. in perspective. If you are staying abroad, you can disseminate the local knowledge and unique practices of your region and make it an innovative and knowledge rich society. Your time contribution may range from one day a week to one hour per day. You can also think of giving certain days per week or a few months. Time is yours, so is your commitment.

How can I become the member of the Network?

You can certainly sign in the guest book with your proposal of volunteering. However, you can also write to us directly at membership@esrokashmir.org for membership queries.

Do I have to pay for the membership fees?

No, there are no such membership fees for joining the Network. However, we would certainly look for a member, who is contributing to the mission of the network in a meaningful way. Even if you dont want to become the member, still you can write to us about you.
