Faunal Biodiversity Inventory of Jammu and Kashmir - PISCES (Fishes)

The available data suggests that 44 species of fishes belonging to 14 genera under 5 families occur in the state. It may be mentioned that trout were introduced many years ago in the state and have thrived well. The other species that probably pave been introduced into ponds and lakes of the state include silver carp (Cirrhinus cirrhosus), carp (Cirrhinus carpio), mirgal (Cirrhinus mrigala), grass carp (Cirrhinus sp.) kalbose (Labeo calbasu), rohu (Labeo rohita) and catla (Catla catla).
