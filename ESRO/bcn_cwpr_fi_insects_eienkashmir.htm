Faunal Biodiversity Inventory of Jammu and Kashmir INSECTS

The available data reveals that as many as 225 species of insects besides several sub-species belonging to 136 genera, 35 families, 4 orders occur in the state.  The analysis at a broad level suggests that Kashmir valley harbors most of the species (about 69 per cent). The analysis of the data on butterflies reveals that about 60 % species occur in the forests below 3000 m, 50 % immediately above upto elevation of about 4200 m, 7 % immediately above upto 5500 m, and less than 3 % at extreme elevations above 5500 m.
