Species Specific Conservation Concern

  • Comprehensive, long-term research of wild animals Kashmir for a better understanding of their biology and ecology for conservation planning and management.

  • A focus on conservation landscapes to support demographically and ecologically viable wildlife metapopulations, efforts to identify their habitat in priority landscapes, and community based monitoring programs.

  • Support a regional initiative to address wildlife trade.

  • Support programs to mitigate the humansnow leopard conflict in the conservation landscapes.

  • Support transboundary dialog through an annual meeting of the wildlife working group to discuss conservation actions and issues, and share lessons.

  • Work closely with other partners and organizations working towards wildlife conservation in the region.

  • Generating greater awareness about threats to wild animals and bringing about changes in attitudes and behavior towards wild animals

  • People's participation in conservation and conflict resolution efforts.

  • Produce a short video to introduce  animal issues for presentation at workshops and TV broadcasts; and

  • Organize a TV panel discussion program featuring a wild life specialist, conservationist, and local officials and celebrities.
