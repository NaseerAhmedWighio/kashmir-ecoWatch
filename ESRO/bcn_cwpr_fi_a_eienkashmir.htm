Faunal Biodiversity Inventory of Jammu and Kashmir Amphibians/ Reptiles

The state is a home to 14 species of amphibians belonging to 6 genera, 5 families and 1 order, and 68 species of reptiles belonging to 43 genera, 12 families and 2 orders. The dominant families in terms of species richness include Colubridae (22 species), Gekonidae (13 species) and Agamidae (6 species). The rest of the families are represented by 1 or 2 species. As many as 53 species of reptiles occur in sub-tropical areas, 19 in temperate regions and 10 in the cold desert ecosystem.
