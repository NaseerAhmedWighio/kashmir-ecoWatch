Services

eIEN KASHMIR

 

 

Our focus is on:

  • Improving environmental performance;

  • Reducing project risk and cost; and

  • Increasing project value.

We supply consultant services for transport, waste, water, energy, industrial, residential and commercial development, commerce and finance, research and policy. We assist both public and private sector clients. We are committed to effective communication and positive management of our supply chain.

Philosophy

  • Partnering : We know from experience that partnering clients is the key to success - to work openly and with joint objectives with our clients, project team partners and stakeholders.

  • Efficiency : Our aim is to save you time, money and potential risk by listening, understanding and developing the right mix of skills for you, and we will continually improve upon our service so that you will come back to us time and again.

  • Innovation : eIEN Kashmir is client focused. Our service development is suited to your needs to help you to meet best practice and legislative requirements whilst meeting and exceeding project goals. We are firmly committed to providing innovative and pragmatic advice to enable positive sustainable development that is beneficial for our clients and our environment.

  • Precision : Because eIEN Kashmir associates with specialists across the Kashmir and internationally, we are flexible, fast acting and very precise in building project teams that are locked-on to your requirements. We can advise you on the latest drivers that affect your interests and can deliver you realistic and sound solutions.

Services

Natural Resources & Ecological Assessment


eIEN Kashmir has extensive experience in ecology covering the full range of surveys required to carry out robust ecological appraisals and assessments. Moreover we use our understanding of the ecology of habitats and species to add value to projects leading to protection of appropriate resources and better designed projects which minimise impacts. eIEN Kashmir has qualified ecologists that provide:

    • Rapid early identification of ecological value and constraints;

    • Baseline ecological survey, assessment and evaluation;

    • Specialist and detailed ecological surveys  protected species including -mammals, reptiles, amphibians, birds, invertebrates and plants;

    • Ecological impact assessment;

    • Mitigation proposals;

    • Expert witness;

    • Habitat restoration;

    • Ecological management plans; and

    • Research and monitoring.

 

Environmental Risk Assessment


The management of environmental risk is integral to the success of our clients business. Issues such as protected species, contaminated land and archaeology can pose a potential hidden risk to client interests. Where clients are acquiring or disposing of properties, we provide early environmental information and pragmatic advice/management options to reduce such risks. We provide experience and reliability in:

    • Environmental opportunities and constraints advice;

    • Rapid environmental risk identification and evaluation;

    • Evaluation of development options; and

    • Due diligence and environmental audit.

Facilitation & Training


Ever changing market drivers and environmental standards have lead to increasing needs for environmental communication, management and reporting. Working with regulatory authorities, stakeholders and communities, eIEN Kashmir has an excellent understanding of national and local environmental and social issues and assists clients in achieving positive environmental performance by providing:

    • Tailored environmental training programmes;

    • Advice on changes in environmental regulations and standards;

    • Public participation strategy and stakeholder facilitation; and

Environmental Management & Solutions
