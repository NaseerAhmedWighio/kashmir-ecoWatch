Supporters

eIEN KASHMIR

 

 

Manadate of eIEN-South Asia, Kashmir is to synerging efforts of International and National organizations besides that of Ministry of Environments - Forests - Wildlife, Fisheries, Urban Environments; programmes related to conservation and protection of environment in Kashmir.

The journey for the eIEN Network has been both adventurous as well as insightful. However, more than anything else, it has been mutually enriching, thanks to all the partner organizations, who constitute the Advocacy Network. Besides the institutional collaborators we have individual collaborators from Overseas Kashmiri's.
